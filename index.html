So what does an average day of a GIS worker look like?
Well, it depends on what your interest is...

You can focus on cartography (map making) if you enjoy design

You can focus on data stewardship and editing if you enjoy raw data

You can focus on software and data development if you enjoy engineering

Or you can focus on spatial analysis if you enjoy science

But the cool thing is that you can mix any of these together!
